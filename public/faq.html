Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing bingo?
Simply sign up for an account, verify your email, and choose a game room. Click "Join" to start playing!
How do I add funds to my account?
Click the "Add Funds" button in your account panel. We accept cryptocurrency payments for secure transactions.
What are power-ups and how do I use them?
Power-ups give you advantages during games like Auto Dab and Extra Ball. Purchase them in the Power-Up Shop and they'll be available during gameplay.
How do tournaments work?
Tournaments are special events where players compete for larger prizes. Entry fees vary, and prizes are distributed based on performance.
Can I play on my mobile device?
Yes! BingoBest is fully responsive and works on all devices. You can even install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for a native app experience.
How do I withdraw my winnings?
Withdrawals are processed through the same cryptocurrency method you used to deposit. Contact support for assistance with withdrawals.
Is my personal information safe?
Absolutely! We use bank-level encryption and never share your personal information. All transactions are secure and anonymous.
What if I have a problem with my account?
Contact our support team at support@betbingo.live. We're available 24/7 to help with any issues.
Can I set spending limits?
Yes! We encourage responsible gaming. You can set daily, weekly, and monthly deposit limits in your account settings.
How do I earn achievements?
Achievements are earned by completing various tasks like winning games, playing for certain amounts of time, or reaching milestones. Check your achievements tab to see progress!
