Responsible Gaming

⚠️ Important: BetBingo is committed to promoting responsible gaming. If you feel you may have a gambling problem, please seek help immediately.

What is Responsible Gaming?

Responsible gaming means enjoying our bingo games in a way that is safe, fun, and within your means. It's about making informed decisions and maintaining control over your gaming activities.

Signs of Problem Gaming

  • Spending more money or time than you can afford
  • Chasing losses with bigger bets
  • Lying about your gaming activities
  • Neglecting work, family, or other responsibilities
  • Feeling anxious or depressed about gaming
  • Borrowing money to play

Our Commitment to You

  • Age Verification: We verify that all players are 18+ years old
  • Deposit Limits: Set daily, weekly, or monthly deposit limits
  • Time Limits: Set session time limits to control play time
  • Self-Exclusion: Take a break from gaming when needed
  • Reality Checks: Regular reminders of time and money spent

Getting Help

If you're concerned about your gaming habits, help is available:

  • National Problem Gambling Helpline: 1-800-522-4700
  • Gamblers Anonymous: www.gamblersanonymous.org
  • National Council on Problem Gambling: www.ncpgambling.org

Setting Limits

You can set the following limits in your account:

  • Daily deposit limit: $10 - $500
  • Weekly deposit limit: $50 - $2,000
  • Monthly deposit limit: $200 - $5,000
  • Session time limit: 15 minutes - 4 hours

Self-Exclusion Options

  • Cooling-off period: 24 hours to 6 weeks
  • Self-exclusion: 6 months to 5 years
  • Permanent exclusion: Permanent account closure
Remember: Gaming should be fun and entertaining. If it stops being fun, it's time to take a break.
